Define boundaries and limitations for the agent's behavior to ensure appropriate and ethical interactions.

Guardrails {{guardrail}} Examples: "Never make medical claims", "Stay within product specifications", "Maintain professional boundaries"
Tips for effective guardrails:
  • Be specific and clear about what the agent should not do
  • Focus on ethical and professional boundaries
  • Include data privacy and security considerations
  • Consider regulatory compliance requirements
  • Define limits of expertise and authority
